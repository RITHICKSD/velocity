Questions

Frequently Asked

What brands do you specialize in?

We specialize in European luxury and exotic vehicles including Porsche, BMW, Mercedes-AMG, Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, Aston Martin, Audi, and more. Our technicians hold factory certifications for all major brands.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes, all our services come with a minimum 24-month / 24,000-mile warranty on parts and labor. Performance tuning packages include a 12-month powertrain warranty.

How long does a typical diagnostic take?

A comprehensive diagnostic scan typically takes 1-2 hours. Complex electrical issues may require additional time. We provide detailed reports with photos and videos of any findings.

Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts?

We exclusively use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts or better. For performance upgrades, we partner with top-tier manufacturers like Akrapovic, Bilstein, and Brembo.

Can you work on electric or hybrid vehicles?

Absolutely. Our facility is equipped with high-voltage safety equipment and our technicians are certified for EV and hybrid systems including Porsche Taycan, BMW iX, and Tesla models.

Do you offer pickup and delivery service?

Yes, we offer complimentary enclosed vehicle transport within a 50-mile radius for all Performance and Bespoke package clients. Contact us for details on our white-glove pickup service.
