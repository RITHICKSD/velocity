About Velocity Motorsports

Precision. Passion. Performance. Our story, our team, our vision.

Our Journey

  • 2010
    Founded

    Velocity Motorsports opens as a boutique performance shop, focused on European sports cars and track prep.

  • 2014
    First Championship

    Our client wins a regional GT championship—our reputation for precision and reliability grows.

  • 2018
    Facility Expansion

    We move into a state-of-the-art, 10,000 sq ft facility with in-house dyno, fabrication, and diagnostics.

  • 2023
    EV & Hybrid Launch

    Certified for high-voltage systems, we begin building and tuning the next generation of performance vehicles.

  • Today
    Trusted by Enthusiasts

    From supercars to classics, Velocity is the destination for those who demand the best.
